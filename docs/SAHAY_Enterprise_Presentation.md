## **1. The Challenge**
In a traditional enterprise, data is like a locked vault. 
*   If a Manager wants to know who was late, they wait for an IT report.
*   If HR wants to check canteen spending, they wait for a monthly audit.
*   If Security needs to track a visitor, they search through logbooks.

**The Barrier**: People speak English; Databases speak SQL.

---

## **2. The Solution: SAHAY Intelligence**
SAHAY acts as the **Executive Receptionist** for your data. It understands conversational English and translates it into technical commands instantly.

### **The "Executive Office" Analogy**
*   **The Receptionist (`Node.js API`)**: Greets the user, takes the request, and makes sure the office is secure.
*   **The Specialist (`Neural Brain`)**: An expert who has studied 3,991 different types of company questions. They look at your question and instantly know which "Data Folder" to pull.
*   **The Translator (`LLM`)**: A language genius who takes the data and turns it into a beautiful, readable answer for you.

## **3. Real-World Use Cases**

### **📍 Case 1: The Punctuality Audit**
**Question**: *"Who from the IT department arrived after 10 AM today?"*
*   **SAHAY’s Logic**: Identifies "IT", "10 AM", and "Today".
*   **The Result**: You get a list of names and their exact punch times in seconds.
*   **Business Value**: Real-time operational discipline without manual Excel sheets.

### **🍱 Case 2: Canteen Cost Control**
**Question**: *"What was the total canteen spend for HR last week?"*
*   **SAHAY’s Logic**: Combines Attendance logs with Canteen transaction logs.
*   **The Result**: A total rupee amount for HR subsidies.
*   **Business Value**: Instant financial oversight of operational costs.

### **🛡️ Case 3: Security & Presence**
**Question**: *"Is there anyone currently in the Server Room?"*
*   **SAHAY’s Logic**: Checks the Live Room Presence view.
*   **The Result**: A list of employees currently occupying that space.
*   **Business Value**: High-level security monitoring for restricted zones.
